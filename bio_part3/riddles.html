BIO EDUCATION RIDDLES

RIDDLES

1. A man rides into town on Friday, he stays three days and leaves on Friday. How does he do it?

2. What two words, added together, contain the most letters?

3. What four-letter word can be written forward, backward, or upside down, and can sill be read from left to right?

4. Two in a corner, one in a room, zero in a house, but one in a shelter. What am I?

5.What belongs to you, but everyone else uses it?

6. What starts with a T, ends with a T, and has T in it?

7. Two fathers and two sons are in a car, yet there are only three people in the car. How?

8. Forwards I am heavy, backward I am not. What am I?

9. I have branches, but no fruit, trunk, or leaves. What am I?

10. Poor people have it. Rich people need it. If you eat it you die. What is it?
